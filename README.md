# Human-Robot Interaction

## Pepper Robot for Hospital Support

### Introduction
The context of this interaction involves a robot operating within a hospital
environment. Specifically, we use the humanoid robot Pepper, which will in-
teract with patients by handling specific, yet impactful, tasks that enhance
service quality while saving hospital staff valuable time. Pepper is responsible
for managing patients’ schedule reminders, such as following the therapeutic
plan defined by doctors or scheduling relatives’ meetings. With prior knowledge
of schedules, the robot delivers clear and precise reminders, relieving staff from
repetitive tasks that otherwise consume precious time. Simultaneously, Pepper
addresses patients’ emotional well-being by helping them cope with stressful
moments or engaging them with interactive games. The goal is to positively
influence their mood, regardless of patient type or condition.
In essence, Pepper functions as an autonomous hospital assistant. It can:
• Exploit past experience memory
• Adapts to individual users during mood regulation activities
• Autonomously requests human intervention at the patient’s request
• Takes the initiative to propose activities to the user
ensuring comprehensive and safe support in a patient-centred care experience.
During interactions, we assume that Pepper is placed in front of the patient
to provide its services. In this task, managing the patient’s emotional state,
providing entertainment, and remembering schedules are the primary objectives.
